Public CVS server:

login:
cvs -d :pserver:uadecvs@zakalwe.virtuaalipalvelin.net:/home/cvs/uade-cvsroot login

To sync uade 1 cvs module, do:

cvs -d :pserver:uadecvs@zakalwe.virtuaalipalvelin.net:/home/cvs/uade-cvsroot -q co uade-2

To sync uade 2 cvs module, do:

cvs -d :pserver:uadecvs@zakalwe.virtuaalipalvelin.net:/home/cvs/uade-cvsroot -q co uade

The password for uadecvs acount is uadecvs.
